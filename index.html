About

Who we are

Inspired by compassion and faith, Al-Noor Trust serves families in Pattoki and surrounding rural areas through education, healthcare and vocational training.

Non-profit, community-led

Established in 2000 by the late Muhammad Shoaib, Al-Noor Trust School provides mixed-gender education from Nursery to 10th grade for children who would otherwise struggle to access quality schooling. We also run initiatives in basic healthcare and skills training so that families can stand on their own feet.

Location: Pattoki, Pakistan
Students: Over 200 enrolled each year
Recognition: Authorized by Boards of Intermediate & Secondary Education
Accountability: Annual audits and transparent reporting

Our core values

  • Dignity: Every child deserves to learn in a safe, respectful environment.
  • Access: We prioritise orphans and families in severe financial need.
  • Opportunity: Education, healthcare and vocational training open doors to long-term change.
  • Trust: Responsible stewardship of donations with audited accounts and clear reporting.
Mission & Vision

What guides our work

Mission

To provide holistic, values-based education and essential services to children and families in need, empowering them with knowledge, skills and opportunities for a dignified life.

Vision

A community where every child learns with joy, every mother has access to basic healthcare, and every family has the skills and support to break the cycle of poverty.
